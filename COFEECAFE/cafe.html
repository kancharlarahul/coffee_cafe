TYPES OF COFFEES

CAPPUCCINO

Cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk including a layer of milk

ESPRESSO

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee produced by forcing hot water under high pressure through coffee.

IRISH

Irish is a caffeinated alcoholic drink consisting of Irish whiskey, hot coffee and sugar, which has been stirred and topped

LATTE

Latte in English, is a coffee drink of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk, traditionally served in a glass.
